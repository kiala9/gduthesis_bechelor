\begin{enabstract}
This document is a LaTeX thesis template for undergraduate graduation design or thesis writing. It provides a directly editable and basically compilable structure that is aligned, as far as possible, with the formatting style shown in the official handbook sample.

The template includes the common components of a thesis, such as cover page, Chinese and English abstracts, table of contents, main chapters, conclusion, references, acknowledgement, and appendix. Users are expected to modify the thesis metadata in the main file first and then replace the sample chapter content with their own work.

\end{enabstract}
